About Us

We're an informal group of avid sewers who like to organise and meet once a week to discuss projects and share stories.

Our meetings take place in the evening and there usually is a good selection of tea and buiscits to share.

We also offer workshops and classes for beginner sewers, so please do leave your information if you would like to know more.
